Frequently asked questions

Is Leonardo.ai the right choice for beginners?

If you’re exploring AI visuals, the interface is approachable and workflows can be learned quickly. Start small, iterate, and consult the official docs when in doubt.

Do I keep rights to the images?

Rights and licensing vary by provider and plan. Review the official terms for usage, attribution, and commercial policies before publishing.

Can I generate consistent characters or products?

Consistency often requires careful prompts, references, or model customization. Expect iterative refinement to reach stable results.

How do I cite or attribute outputs?

Check the provider’s attribution guidance and your client’s requirements. When sharing process shots, label AI-assisted steps for clarity.

What about data privacy?

Avoid submitting confidential or personal data. Review how prompts and outputs may be stored or used to improve services.

Does it replace designers?

AI can accelerate ideation and production, but human direction and quality control remain essential—especially for brand work.
